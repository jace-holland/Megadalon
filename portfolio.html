Heavy Fuel Oil

Heavy fuel oil (HFO) is a low-quality fuel made from the residue of crude oil distillation. It is commonly used in power generation and marine applications due to its high energy content and low cost.

Mineral oils are lubricating oils that are refined from naturally occurring crude oil. They are composed of 80–90% petroleum hydrocarbon distillate with 10–20% additives to impart specific properties to the oil.
